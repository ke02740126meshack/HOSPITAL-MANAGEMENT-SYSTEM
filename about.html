About MedAxis

Transforming Healthcare Through Innovation Since 2011

Our Story

Founded in 2011, MedAxis began with a simple mission: to make healthcare management simpler, smarter, and more accessible for medical professionals worldwide. What started as a small clinic management tool has evolved into a comprehensive AI-powered healthcare platform.

Today, we're trusted by over 5,000 clinics and 8,000+ doctors across 6 continents. Our team of healthcare professionals, engineers, and data scientists work together to create solutions that truly understand the needs of modern medical practices.

We believe technology should enhance the doctor-patient relationship, not complicate it. That's why we've built a platform that's powerful yet intuitive, comprehensive yet easy to use.
